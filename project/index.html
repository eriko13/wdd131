About Camilo

Camilo is a 3-year-old Chihuahua mix who came into my life and changed it forever. With his expressive eyes and curious nature, he's more than just a pet—he's family.

Known for his playful antics and loyal companionship, Camilo enjoys long walks, playing fetch, and curling up on the couch for movie nights. His favorite activities include chasing squirrels in the park and greeting everyone who visits our home.

Camilo's Favorite Activities

Outdoor Adventures

Camilo loves exploring new environments, from parks to hiking trails. Despite his small size, he's an adventurous pup who enjoys discovering new scents and sounds.

Playtime

Whether it's a squeaky toy or a game of fetch, Camilo is always ready to play. His energy and enthusiasm during playtime never fail to bring joy to everyone around him.

Cozy Naps

After a day of adventures, Camilo enjoys curling up in his favorite spot for a nap. He's particularly fond of warm blankets and sunny patches on the couch.

Camilo may be small in size, but he's huge in personality and love. He reminds me daily that the best things in life often come in small packages.

— Erik, Camilo's Human
