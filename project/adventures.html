Camilo's Adventures

Park Exploration

June 15, 2023

Our local dog park has become Camilo's favorite weekend destination. Today's visit was particularly special as Camilo made friends with a Golden Retriever twice his size!

Despite being a small Chihuahua mix, Camilo has never been intimidated by larger dogs. In fact, he seems to prefer playing with dogs much bigger than himself. His confidence never ceases to amaze me.

First Beach Day

July 22, 2023

Camilo experienced his first beach day this summer, and it was a day full of new discoveries and experiences!

Initially, Camilo was cautious about the sand, testing it gently with his paws and looking at me with confusion. But it didn't take long for him to realize this new terrain was perfect for running and digging.

Mountain Trail

August 10, 2023

Many people underestimate what small dogs like Chihuahuas can do, but Camilo proved them all wrong on our hiking trip to Sunset Mountain!

We started early in the morning to avoid the heat, with Camilo excitedly leading the way from the moment we hit the trail. His tiny legs moved twice as fast as mine, but his enthusiasm never waned throughout the hike.
